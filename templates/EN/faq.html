Is D-dagen 2020 happening?

Yes, but due to the current circumstances D-dagen 2020 will be the computer science chapters first digital fair!

When is D-dagen 2020 taking place?

November 5th.

Will there be dedicated rooms for students to virtually participate in the fair?

Yes, we will book rooms on campus where students will be able to, uninterruptedly, videochat with the companies. We will also have a D-dagen lounge with dedicated spots for virtual participation in the fair.

Will we hand out merch?

Yes, we will have a D-dagen lounge on campus where we will arrange mini competitions and hand out goodiebags and company merch. We would gladly see that participating companies send in merch that we can hand out.

What is D-dagen?

D-Dagen is the KTH Computer Science chapter’s yearly job fair. This year, the event takes place during 10th October 2019. The event is aimed toward providing companies and students within the Computer Science and IT sector an opportunity to meet and get to know each other. The project group behind D-Dagen wants to help establish a more direct and personal contact between company representatives and students.

How many companies have participated in D-Dagen previous years?

  • 2018: 75
  • 2017: 59
  • 2016: 45
  • 2015: 35

How many visitors usually attend D-Dagen?

Last year D-Dagen had 2000 visitor.

Who is D-dagen for?

D-Dagen is for students active within the Computer Science and IT sector.

Why should my company participate in D-Dagen?

D-Dagen is an outstanding opportunity for Computer Science and IT companies to connect with students in different stages in their studies. Maybe you are looking for Master thesis students, part time employees, summer internships, or newly graduated MSc or PhD students? Do you want to boost your visibility as an employer, showcase your ongoing projects or share your corporate culture? Then don’t hesitate to apply to D-Dagen!

What else is there to do in connection to D-Dagen?

There is a possibility of arranging company events in connection to D-Dagen. Please contact d-dagen.event@d.kth.se for more information.

What is the D-Dagen catalogue and where can I find the catalogues from previous years?

The D-Dagen catalogue is given out to visitors in connection with the event every year. It is a collection of the most important information about the fair as well as information about the participating companies. The catalogue contains a map over the placement of the company booths. This year the catalogue will be available in an electronic format at ddagen.se and only a condensed brochure will be printed in order to minimize our climate impact. The electronic catalogue will also allow for search- and filter functions will increase the usability.
