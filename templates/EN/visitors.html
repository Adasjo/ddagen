Welcome to D‑DAGEN 2019


We are happy to invite you to visit D-Dagen 2019! Every year many interesting companies and agencies within the computer science and IT sector visit D-Dagen to network with students. Last year, there were over 70 companies participating in D-Dagen.

Right now the project group is working hard to make this year's fair as great as possible. On this page you will find helpful information for visitors of the fair, such as an interactive map over the fair venue with a filtering functions. We aim to bring you a smooth and painless experience before, during and after the career fair. More information will be posted continously, so stay updated!

Hälsning från D-Dagenansvariga


Together with this year’s excited project group we wish to welcome you to D-Dagen 2019! We continuously work to improve D-Dagen to make it as great as possible for everyone who is studying computer science at KTH. For us, this means bringing interesting events around the fair and broaden the range to create more value for the students who participate.

We have also continued our efforts with getting a good distribution of companies from different business segments in order to create a fair where students and companies can meet in a simple way regardless of how far along you are in your studies! In addition, the size of the fair has continued to increase and we are larger than ever before with a total of 79 companies attending the fair.

We in the project group hope to see you at D-Dagen 2019!
