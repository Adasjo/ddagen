APPLICATION

The application process to exhibit on D-Dagen 2020 contains three steps:

STEP 1

The first step is to fill out the interest form. After the form is submitted you will receive an email informing you if you have received a spot at the fair. If you have been allocated a spot, an agreement will be attached in the email.

Interest Form 2020

STEP 2

The next step is to sign the contract (including the order information form), scan it within three (3) weeks and send it to d‑dagen@d.kth.se with the title “Contract D-Dagen 2020 - [Company name]”. Please note that if we do not receive the contract within 3 weeks from the date the contract is sent, your spot in the fair will be removed.

STEP 3

During the last step you will receive a signed contract from D-Dagen within 2 weeks from the date we received the contract. The application process is thus completed.

Signed contract - What happens now?


  • We create a brochure which is handed out to students before the fair. To ensure that you are in the brochure fill out the following form so that we have your company logo and relevant information. If you have already filled in the form you do not have to do it again.
  • Week 42 you will have access to the platform, and will from then on be able to start creating your company page.
  • We will also in week 42 send out instructions in the form of PDF:s and shorter videos on how to use the platform.
  • Furthermore in week 42 we will hold two similar webinars during two different time slots. During the webinar we will show you how to use the platform. The webinar will be recorded so that you can watch it afterwards in case you cannot be available during the given time slots. The date and times for the webinars are:
    • 16 october, 10.00 - 11.00
    • 16 october, 15.00 - 16.00
  • We will hand out company merch (e.g. pens, water bottles, t-shirts) during D-dagen. If you want us to hand out your company merch we need to receive it at the following address : “Osquars backe 21, 100 44, Stockholm”, either the 3rd or 4th of november between 10:00-17:00.
  • We will during the D-dagen week hand out goodiebags to students. If you want your company logo to be on these goodiebags, or in any other way want to sponsor the goodiebag eller to the fair itself with e.g. coffee mugs with your logo, you can send an e-mail to sponsorship@ddagen.se.
  • D-dagen takes place on the 5th of november between 10:00 and 16:00, and during that time you will be able to chat and have video calls with students.

Events

If you want extra visibility for your company before D-dagen a perfect way to do this is to arrange events with the computer science chapter the days or the week before D-dagen. If you want to arange a lunch lecture or some other event you can contact NLG (the computer science chapters business group) at foretag@d.kth.se. State that you want to arrange an event in connection to D-dagen and they will handle the communication with you surrounding the event

We wish you a warm welcome to D-dagen 2020!
